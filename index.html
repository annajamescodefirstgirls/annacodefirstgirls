The Exeter Bucket List

Everything you need to do before you graduate

Go to Firehouse
The inspiration behind The Leaky Cauldron in Harry Potter. Order one of their legendary dustbin-lid pizzas at 2am and wash it down with a toffee cider.

Order a 30" Pizza at On the Waterfront
When there are too many amazing topping combinations to choose from why not got for 3 on one pizza! Although you'll need some friends to help!

Win The Vic's Pub Quiz
Test your general knowledge in every student in Pennsylvania's favourite local pub.

Take part in Rock Solid
There's no better sense of achievement than completing a freezing cold muddy obstacle course. It's also loads of fun!

Hitchhike for Charity
RAG run loads of events such as the Hitchhike to Amsterdam, Jailbreak and Lost - not only fun experience to look back on but also raising money for charity.

Go to Ottery St Mary on Bonfire Night
There's nothing more unique than watching people running through the streets carrying flaming barrels of tar.

Stay until lights on at the Lemmy
No better way to spend a Saturday night than dancing to Angels in Exeter's biggest club.

Visit Exmouth
One of the best things about Exeter is having the beach on your doorstep.

Eat a Scone at Tea on the Green
Go for a classic Devon cream tea right on the Cathedral Green - the scones here are second to none.

Ride the Pedaloes at the Quay
The perfect afternoon with your friends in the post-exam sunshine. Just watch out for swans!

Graduate
The hard work will all be worth it! Celebrate in the Forum with friends and family, glass of bubbly and legendary graduation cupcakes!
